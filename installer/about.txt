SofaControl - Xbox controller as a couch remote for your PC
============================================================

SofaControl turns an Xbox controller into a full virtual mouse and keyboard,
so you can run your living-room / home-theater PC from the sofa without ever
reaching for a real keyboard or mouse.

What it does
------------
  * Move the mouse, click, scroll and zoom with the controller.
  * Pop up an on-screen keyboard to type, search and log in.
  * Alt+Tab between apps, go back/forward in the browser.
  * One combo flips into "game mode": your controller instantly works as a
    real Xbox pad inside games and apps.
  * Extra combos can close the current game, or put the PC to sleep / shut it
    down - all from the controller.

Perfect for couch gaming, streaming video, music and general media-center use.

What gets installed
-------------------
To make all of this work, the setup also installs two small, open-source,
signed drivers from Nefarius Software Solutions:

  * ViGEmBus - lets SofaControl present a virtual Xbox 360 controller to games.
  * HidHide  - hides your real controller from games so only SofaControl sees
               it (no double inputs).

If you already have either driver, the setup will simply skip it. A reboot may
be requested after a first-time driver install.

Open source
-----------
SofaControl is free and open source (MIT License). You can read the code,
build it yourself, suggest changes, or just enjoy it. If it saves you a few
trips to the keyboard, there is a "Buy me a coffee" button inside the app.
